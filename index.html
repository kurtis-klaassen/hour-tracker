Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

HourTracker ("we", "our", "the app") is a time tracking application designed for trades workers and their employers. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address, full name, and account type (worker or boss). When you use the app, we store your time entries including clock in/out times, break durations, job names, categories, and notes you add.

If you enable location tracking, we collect GPS coordinates when you clock in or out. If you attach photos, those images are stored with your time entries. All data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database service.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the time tracking service. Time entries, locations, and photos are stored so you can review your work history and share it with your employer if you choose to link your account. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Boss-Worker Data Sharing

If you are a worker and accept an invitation from a boss, that boss will be able to view your time entries, including hours, job names, categories, notes, attached photos, and location data. You can disconnect from a boss at any time through the app settings. Bosses cannot modify your entries.

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase. Data is transmitted using HTTPS encryption. Access to your data is protected by your account credentials. We implement row-level security policies to ensure users can only access data they are authorized to view.

Your Rights

You can view all data associated with your account within the app. You can edit or delete individual time entries at any time. You can delete your entire account and all associated data permanently through the app settings. Account deletion removes all your entries, jobs, categories, and profile information.

Location Data

Location tracking is optional and controlled by you. You can enable or disable automatic location tracking in the app settings. When enabled, GPS coordinates are captured only at the moment of clocking in or out. We do not track your location in the background or at any other time.

Photos

Photos you attach to time entries are stored with your account data. You can delete attached photos at any time by editing the entry. Photos are only visible to you and, if connected, your boss.

Children's Privacy

HourTracker is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us through the app or via the contact information provided on our Google Play Store listing.
